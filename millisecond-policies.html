Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data Millisecond ("the App") collects and how it's used. The App is developed by Hassaan Maqsood ("I", "the developer").

Data collected directly by the App

None. The App does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email, or any personal information, and does not run its own servers. Your level, high score, and reaction times are stored only on your device and are not sent anywhere.

Third-party services

The App currently does not use ads, analytics, or in-app purchases, and does not integrate any third-party SDK that collects data. It is completely free with no monetization.

This may change in a future update — for example, ads or an in-app purchase may be added later. If that happens, this policy and the Data Safety section below will be updated first, and the "Last updated" date will reflect the change.

Sharing your score

The "Share Score" feature opens your device's native share menu. Nothing is sent to the developer — the App simply hands off text to whichever app you choose (Messages, WhatsApp, etc.).

Data retention and deletion

No personal data is collected or stored by the App, so there is nothing to request or delete from the developer. To clear locally stored data (level, high score), uninstall the App or clear its storage from your device settings.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
